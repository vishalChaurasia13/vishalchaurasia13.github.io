Car-Rental System

A Car-Rental System is divided into two parts: the admin part and the client side. In the admin section, there is a login interface where administrators can log in using their email or mobile number and password. The dashboard features three options in the navbar: category, subcategories, and vehicles, where data is stored. I implemented CRUD operations to add, update, and delete cars from the dashboard. On the client side, users first select the date, time, and location, and all available cars are displayed on the website. Users can employ dynamic filtering to refine their car search based on fuel type, brand, and category. When a client clicks on a specific car, detailed information is shown on a separate page, including a booking option. After entering necessary details and receiving an OTP, the client proceeds with the booking. Payment is made using Razorpay, offering options such as UPI and credit card. Once the payment is completed, the selected car is booked for the client.
